What We Believe About Giving

There are truly only three things we can give: our time, our treasure or our talents; each of these are a gift from God and like we read in the Old Testament God has made us stewards, caretakers, of these gifts. As Christians we recognize that these gifts can be used to support the mission of spreading God’s Word and sharing God’s Love in our community and to all the ends of the earth. The Bible has a lot to say about giving and so it is a subject that requires careful consideration, but at the center of this we know that God loves a cheerful giver and so each should give without reluctance or under compulsion.
