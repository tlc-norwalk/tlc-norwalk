Meet the Team

Pastor Eddie Cuen

Lead Pastor

Pastor Eddie has been married to his wife, Denise, since 1995 and is a proud father of two adult children. He was born in Ensenada, Mexico and has lived in the Los Angeles area most of his life. In 2012 he enrolled in the Specific Ministry Pastoral Program through Concordia Seminary, Saint Louis and was ordained in 2015. Before his life as a pastor, Eddie worked for 17 years in the fashion industry where he worked in design, product development, production and merchandising. He is passionate about sharing the love of Christ with the community and enjoys running marathons.

Dan Malone

Worship Leader

Dan has had a love for Jesus since he was very young. Originally from the Chicago area he moved to Arizona after high school and eventually ended up in California. He is a self-taught guitarist and keyboard player and loves to sing. He began leading worship at his campus church at Arizona State University in 1977. He continues to serve the Lord preaching the Gospel through music through leading worship and writing songs. Dan has been married to his wife Janet since he met her at church in 1988. He is also a proud father of 5 children and 8 grandchildren.
